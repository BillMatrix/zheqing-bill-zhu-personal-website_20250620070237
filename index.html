About Me

I am the Founder and CEO of Pokee AI, where we're building the next generation of AI agents powered by reinforcement learning. I earned my Ph.D. from Stanford University under the guidance of Professor Benjamin Van Roy, focusing on reinforcement learning algorithms.

Prior to founding Pokee AI, I worked at Meta AI where I developed applied reinforcement learning solutions, led the Pearl platform, and contributed significantly to revenue generation and advertiser onboarding processes.

My research interests span reinforcement learning, recommendation systems, and multi-agent systems, with a particular focus on developing algorithms that can learn and adapt in complex, real-world environments.

Reinforcement Learning Philosophy

I believe reinforcement learning is crucial for the next generation of AI agents. Unlike traditional machine learning approaches, RL enables agents to learn through interaction with their environment, making decisions that maximize long-term rewards.

This approach is essential for strategic planning and effective task execution in AI agents, allowing them to adapt to changing conditions and improve over time.

At Pokee AI, we're leveraging the power of reinforcement learning to create agents that can understand complex goals, break them down into manageable tasks, and execute them efficiently across various platforms.
